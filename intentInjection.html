    Intent Injection¶

    Intent Injection Examples¶

    We found several examples of Intent injection. We observe a few interesting patterns.

    1. App name: com.fcbh.dbp.BibleSocietyOfPhilippines; Source: Google Play

      Detected Problem: The attacker injects an “url” string into the app through an Intent, and the app accesses the “url” later. (More Details)

    2. App name: com.kamagames.notepad; Source: Google Play

      Detected Problem: The destination of an ICC depends on an incoming intent controlled by the attacker. (More Details)
